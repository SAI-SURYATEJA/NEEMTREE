About us

Established in 2005, NEEMTREE offers comprehensive coaching in IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, GRE, GMAT, German, French, and Spoken English. We excel in guiding students to over 700 universities and colleges across the USA, Canada, UK, Ireland, Europe, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, and Dubai, assisting over 10,000 successful placements. Our services encompass career counselling, university selection, admission procedures, scholarships, financial aid, interview prep, visa support, forex services, air travel, and insurance. As authorized Test Registration Centers for IELTS through IDP Australia and British Council, we also provide German Language Coaching with native instructors. We're proud members of the World Education Association, includi ng ICEF, WEBA, and EAIE.
