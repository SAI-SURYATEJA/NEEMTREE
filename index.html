About us

Established in 2005, NEEMTREE offers coaching in IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, GRE, GMAT, German, French, and Spoken English. We guide students to 700+ universities worldwide, with 10,000+ successful placements. Services include career counseling, university selection, admissions, scholarships, visa support, and more. As authorized Test Registration Centers for IELTS, we're proud members of the World Education Association.
